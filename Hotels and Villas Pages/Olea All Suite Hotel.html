What makes it Kobu
The minimalist rooms at Olea Suite Hotel ooze a romantic ambience. Bohemian style meets modern chic. The muted grey tones and elegant lines work in absolute harmony with the green vegetation outside. As soon as you step foot into your room you feel a sense of calm. This is truly a space to unwind.
Olea try their best to provide good quality, organic products. From the food served in their restaurant to the eco-friendly cleaning products used to clean the rooms. The effort shows and it benefits all of us. There's also a fleet of bicycles at the hotel that you're welcome to use. Not just to save the environment but because it's the best way to explore the area.
