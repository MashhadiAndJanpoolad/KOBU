MÝKONOS, GREECE
The Wild
This unpretentious bohemian retreat tumbles effortlessly down the hillside toward the magical (and private) Kalafatis beach, offering unmatched seclusion to the guests of its 37 rooms.

Photos courtesy of The Wild

Blessed with its own beach on the most cosmopolitan island of Greece the WILD offers privacy and seclusion, the new “luxuries” we seek while traveling in these times. A perfect vacation spot where one can feel relaxed and safe. Situated on a cliffside, the hotel overlooks the deep blue of the Aegean and a small cove once home to “the Wild Ones”, the most fearless fishermen of Mykonos. Now, a tranquil, picturesque port that brings to light how Mykonos used to look like. Simple, raw, beautiful and wild.

What makes it Kobu
It’s the location, a natural amphitheatre on a cliffside over Kalafatis beach. It is the natural textures and the organic forms, the eclectic mix of design, the scent from the evening flowers and the colors from the bougainvilleas as they blend with the plaster and the local stone.
Embracing the slow food philosophy the tastes at the Wild are all about delicious food, local produce and fresh ingredients cooked slowly with traditional techniques.
