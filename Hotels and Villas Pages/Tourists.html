Tourists
This former ’60s-era Redwood Motel, has been painstakingly renovated to it’s Berkshire glory complete with high-end amenities and a visual sensibility inspired by the celebrated design oasis of Sea Ranch, California.

Tourists is a hotel and riverside retreat inspired by the classic American roadside motor lodge, set on the banks of the Hoosic River in the Berkshires city of North Adams. This 48-room property is a union of design and nature, home to woodland trails, riverbank vistas, sculptural installations, and more.

What makes it Kobu
If you are looking for a fun getaway, Tourists has it all. Explore MASS MoCA, hang out with alpacas and cows on a local farm, or just spend all day playing games & eating freshly baked cookies at the hotel.
The beautifully designed lobby has some serious chalet vibes and is a perfect place to gather with family & friends. Using common, organic materials, your room is both haven and trailhead, connecting you with your vacation self and serving as a basecamp for adventure.
