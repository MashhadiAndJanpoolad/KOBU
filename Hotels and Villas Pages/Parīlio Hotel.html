PAROS, GREECE
Parīlio Hotel
Perched on the northeastern coast of Paros, and inspired by Cycladic simplicity, Parīlio’s soothing neutrals, exemplary architecture and luxurious suites offer the perfect low-key hideaway.
BOOK NOW

Photos courtesy of Parīlio Hotel

Athens-based Interior Design Laboratorium was influenced by both the island’s distinctive architecture and their own brand of holistic design when creating the visual concept of the 33-suite Parīlio, the name of which is derived from ‘Paros’ and the Greek word for the sun ‘ilios’. The cycladic art inspiration and influences are reflected throughout the property, just like on its logo.

What makes it Kobu
Enticing many a trendy traveller, the sublime 33-suite Parīlio echoes the surrounding island’s distinctive block white architecture style, while also crafting understated luxury from natural stone materials. The soothing color palette, comprised of neutral white, grays, ochre, ivory, and sienna is sure to entice you into this completely tranquil environment.
Parillo’s amazing pool is highlighted by two impressive rocks and bound by the extraordinary Cycladic natural environment which dominates Parīlio’s outdoor space. For those looking to fully embrace the slow-paced Cycladic way of living, the on-site Asian spa offers the ultimate time out, while the fitness centre is the perfect spot for the sporty types to spend their mornings.
