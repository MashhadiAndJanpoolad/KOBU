Campos Polanco
Nestled in the heart of Polanco, this iconic Art Deco building has been elegantly restored into a 12 suite cosmopolitan residence & social gathering house with a refreshing take on hospitality.

With architectural design by All Arquitectura, conceptual design by AvroKo and interior design by Amass & G, each distinct room and common space masterfully blends the building’s rich architectural heritage with top-of-the-line modern amenities. Guests can enjoy the endless comforts of their private suites, relax in the common spaces on each floor, and admire the Mexican art & design that covers every corner of the residence.

What makes it Kobu
Located on Campos Eliseos, a charming street surrounded by pleasant greenways such as Lincoln Park and Republica Del Líbano Garden, Campos Polanco is a stones throw away from some of Mexico City’s best shops (Habitation 116) and restaurants (Pujol and Quintonil). Take a stroll through the park and enjoy the views of the city's endless greenery.
Campos Polanco works to cultivate a strong sense of community and is a generous supporter of local artisans. Its stunning communal gathering spaces and residential nature of the property lends itself to curating intimate and exclusive experiences. From bespoke events to elegant art openings, Campos has established itself as an advocate of connection.
