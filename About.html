About Homes & Villas by KOBU

What is Homes & Villas by Marriott Bonvoy?

Hotels & Villas by Kobu is a global home rental offering launched in 2019 by Kobu with a vision to bring Marriott’s 90+ years of hospitality expertise to the home rental market. With a focus only on the premium and luxury tier of rental homes, Marriott works with select professional home management companies to ensure that every home listed can be serviced at a standard expected of Kobu.


How is Hotels & Villas by Boku different than the other experiences?

● Highly-Curated Offering: We only work with professional management companies offering premium and luxury private home inventory and each home is evaluated – either in person or digitally - before being listed on our site.
● Professionally Managed Homes Only: Standards matter and each property listed on Homes & Villas by Marriott Bonvoy is professionally managed by one of our trusted property management partners, who provide guests a professionally cleaned home with 24/7 local support team for assistance while staying at the booked home or villa, high-speed Wi-Fi, premium linens and amenities, and family friendly conveniences upon request.
● Earn & Redeem with Marriott Bonvoy: Marriott Bonvoy members can earn and redeem points all stays with Homes & Villas by Marriott Bonvoy. For more details around loyalty members benefits staying at Homes & Villas by Marriott Bonvoy please review the Marriott Bonvoy Benefits section of this FAQ.


Searching & Booking With Us

How can I search for a place to stay?

Searching for a place to stay is simple! You can either search by destination and dates, or you can browse our global offerings through our Hotels & Villas pages.
You can also select filters based on your preferences such as number of guests, number of bedrooms, price, pool and more!


When will you have reviews of your Accomodation?

Hotels & Villas by Kobu is a new offering, which means we do not have reviews from our guests at this time. As more of our guests experience these homes, we will be introducing reviews. However, we would love your feedback. You will receive a survey after your stay that we encourage you to complete to best understand your experience.


Are any other languages supported besides English on the site?

Currently, our site and support are offered only in English. We plan to add additional languages as we continue to expand our inventory around the world.


Payments, Pricing & Reservation Requirements

What are the acceptable methods of payment?

Major credit cards and Kobu points are the current acceptable method of payments.
Kobu Free Night Certificates or gift cards are not accepted.


What happens if my credit card is declined? How would I know?

Can I change my method of payment after booking?

No, this functionality is not currently available. Please be sure to use a form of payment to secure your reservation that will be valid until after you complete your stay.
If you book with a credit card and we are not able to collect your second deposit for your stay, you will be emailed a link to submit payment within 72 hours. If payment is not received within 72 hours, your reservation will be cancelled.


Can I pay for the entire cost of my home reservation in one payment or before second half is due?

This is dependent on the home’s payment & cancellation policy. Please review those policies in the Reservations & Cancellation section below and be sure to check the individual property listing to see which applies to your home.


How do I pay for additional services i.e. pet fee, cart passes, pool fees?

Should you request any services or add-ons that require ancillary fees or charges, these charges will be handled by the home management company after your reservation has been confirmed.



Can I call someone if I don’t want to submit my payment online?

Our site currently only allows for online payment.


Paying with KOBU Points

Where can I find how many points I have?

When you sign in to the Homes & Villas by Kobu website, you are able to see the available Kobu points you have in your account. Simply click on your profile on the upper right-hand corner of the page.

You can also review your account balance by logging into the KOBU site


How do I pay with points?

First sign in to the Hotels & Villas by Kobu website with your Kobu log in information. Once you “Choose Your Dates” on a listing, select “Use Points”. Your account balance and membership identification will be available for review.Once you have logged into your Kobu account you can proceed as normal to select your destination and travel dates.


What happens if I don’t have enough points to cover the entire cost?

If you have not signed in to the Hotels & Villas by Kobu website or if you do not have sufficient Kobu points to secure the reservation, you will not see the “Use Points” button. The “Use Points” button will only be displayed for booking options where you have enough points available.


Am I able to use both Points and cash to pay for one stay?

Yes, you can enjoy the flexibility of booking using the Cash & Points payment option. Please see the “How Do I Make a Reservation using Cash & Points on Kobu?” question for further details.

Travel Insurance

Just like you have car and homeowners insurance to protect your investments, Allianz Travel Insurance can keep your travel budget safe from the unexpected—giving you peace of mind throughout your entire trip.



What benefits may be included in a travel insurance plan?

● Trip Interruption Protection

Provides reimbursement for the unused, non-refundable portions of your trip if it is interrupted due to a covered illness, injury, and more. This can also reimburse additional transportation expenses needed for your early return home or additional home rental nights if you and your party need to quarantine.


● Trip Cancellation Protection

Provides reimbursement for prepaid, non-refundable home rental reservations, plane tickets and change fees, activity costs, and other travel expenses if you need to cancel your trip due to a covered illness, injury, and more.


● Emergency Medical and Dental

Provides reimbursement for expenses related to covered emergency medical or dental care that are incurred on your trip, like if you break your ankle or throw out your back.


● Baggage and Personal Belongings Protection

Provides reimbursement if your personal items are lost, stolen or damaged at any time during your trip. Reimbursable items may include suitcases, cell phones, purses and wallets, and more.


● Concierge Services

Enjoy having your very own personal assistance that is only a phone call away. An award-winning team of travel experts is available to help with experience enhancements like finding tickets to shows, making Hotel Reservations, and booking tee times.

Reservations and Cancellation

How can I cancel my reservation?

Reservations can be canceled on the Hotels & Villas website using the Lookup Reservation function.

Please use the Reservation Confirmation number that begins with the letter ‘M or H’ followed by a series of numbers, and prior to cancelling, please carefully review the cancellation policy for your reservation. If you have any trouble, please contact the relevant home management company introduced to you at the time of booking to assist in processing your cancellation.
