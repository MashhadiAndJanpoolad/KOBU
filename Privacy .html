PRIVACY POLICY

'This Is Nomad LTD’ trading as ‘Kobu’ (“we”) are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle the personal information created when accessing and using our services, how we use any personal information we collect about you and why, what we do with the information and how we share it.
Please read the following carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data. By providing personal data to us, you are accepting and consenting to the practices described in this policy.

Information we collect

When accessing our services, we collect information to enable us to process your orders and improve our service. Information such as name, address, phone number and email address are provided by you when completing registration. We will also collect information such as IP addresses, domain name and referring URL, time of visit, pages visited, and any searches performed. We may also receive and share information about you with third parties we are working with such as external accountants and IT support providers.

Use of Information
We use information held about you in the following ways:

For the provision of services requested by you
To help us identify and provide information on services/products which may be of interest to you
To notify you about changes to our service
To administer and improve our site
To keep our site safe and secure

We may share your Personal data with other organisations in the following ways:

In the event our assets are acquired by a third party, in which case personal data held by us will be one of the transferred assets
If we are under a duty to disclose or share your Personal Data to comply with any legal obligation
Data processors who handle your data on our behalf in accordance with our instructions.



Cookies

Our website uses Cookies to ensure we provide a good experience when visiting our site. Our policy regarding Cookies is listed in the footer.
We embed a Facebook widget to allow you to see number of likes/shares/recommends and “like/share/recommend” our webpages. This widget may collect your IP address, your web browser User Agent, store and retrieve cookies on your browser, embed additional tracking, and monitor your interaction with the widget, including correlating your Facebook account with whatever action you take within the widget (such as “liking/sharing/recommending” our webpage), if you are logged in to Facebook. For more information about how this data may be used, please see Facebook’s data privacy policy: https://www.facebook.com/about/privacy/update

We use a Twitter Tweet widget at our website. As a result, our website makes requests to Twitter’s servers for you to be able to tweet our webpages using your Twitter account. These requests make your IP address visible to Twitter, who may use it in accordance with their data privacy policy: https://twitter.com/en/privacy#update

We use a GooglePlus widget at our website. As a result, our website makes requests to Google’s servers for you to be able to share our webpages using your GooglePlus account. These requests make your IP address visible to Google, who may use it in accordance with their data privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Security

We take reasonable administrative and electronic measures to protect your information. Our websites use SSL which provides encryption, authentication and message integrity. Access to stored data is password protected and access to passwords is limited. We work with our authorising banks and business partners to ensure the security of your credit card information by aligning with PCI requirements.

Policy updates: Any changes or updates to our Privacy Policy will be posted on this page.

GDPR statement: We are committed to compliance with GDPR which came into effect on 25th May 2018.
